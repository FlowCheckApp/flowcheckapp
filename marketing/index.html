Security & Privacy

Your data belongs
to you — always.

We take privacy as seriously as your bank does. Every layer of FlowCheck is built with security-first principles.

🔒

256-bit AES Encryption

All your financial data is encrypted at rest and in transit using bank-grade AES-256 encryption.

🤝

Plaid Secure Connection

Your bank credentials go directly to Plaid — we never see, store, or touch your login or password.

👁️

Face ID / Biometric Lock

FlowCheck locks automatically when you leave the app and requires biometric authentication to re-enter.

🚫

We Never Sell Your Data

Your financial information is never sold to advertisers, data brokers, or third parties. Period.

🗑️

Right to Erasure

Delete your account at any time and all your data — including Plaid connections — is permanently erased within 30 days.

📋

CCPA & GDPR Compliant

Full compliance with California Consumer Privacy Act and GDPR. You control what we collect and how long we keep it.
