Security & trust

Your money stays
completely safe.

FlowCheck is built from the ground up with security as the first priority. We use the same standards as major banks — because your financial data deserves nothing less.

🔒

256-bit AES encryption

All data is encrypted in transit with TLS 1.3 and at rest with AES-256. The same standard used by major financial institutions worldwide.

👁

Read-only bank access

FlowCheck uses Plaid for bank connectivity. We can only read your transactions — we cannot initiate payments, transfers, or any changes to your accounts. Ever.

📱

iOS Keychain storage

Sensitive credentials are stored in the iOS Keychain — hardware-backed, encrypted storage that even FlowCheck cannot read without your biometric or device passcode.

🏦

Powered by Plaid

Bank connections are handled entirely by Plaid, a financial data infrastructure company trusted by over 12,000 apps including Venmo, Robinhood, and Betterment.

🛡

Firebase authentication

User accounts are secured by Google Firebase Authentication, with rate limiting, brute-force protection, and token revocation on suspicious activity.

🔑

Face ID & biometric lock

FlowCheck locks automatically and requires Face ID or Touch ID to unlock. The app blurs your content immediately when you switch apps — before iOS takes its screenshot.
