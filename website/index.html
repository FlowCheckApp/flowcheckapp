Security

Your data is safe. Full stop.

We take privacy as seriously as your bank does.

🔒
Read-only access only. FlowCheck can see your balances and transactions — nothing else. We cannot move money, make payments, or change anything in your accounts.
🔐

256-bit encryption

All data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest. Your bank credentials are handled exclusively by Plaid — we never see them.

🏦

Powered by Plaid

The same bank connection technology used by Venmo, Coinbase, and thousands of other fintech apps. Trusted by millions.

🚫

We never sell your data

Your financial data is yours. We don't sell it, share it with advertisers, or use it to train AI models — ever.

🗑️

Delete anytime

Request full deletion from Settings at any time. Every piece of your data is permanently erased within 30 days.

🔒

SSN never stored

If you check your Experian credit score, your SSN is transmitted directly to Experian over an encrypted connection and never stored by FlowCheck.

📋

FCRA compliant

Credit score requests are consumer-initiated under FCRA § 604(a)(2) — you're pulling your own report, not being underwritten for lending.

FAQ

Common questions

Is FlowCheck really free?
Yes. The core app — bank sync, transactions, bill tracking, and your financial health score — is completely free with one bank connection. Pro ($4.99/month or $34.99/year) unlocks unlimited banks, credit score, and push alerts. No credit card required to start.
Is my banking data safe?
Your bank credentials go directly to Plaid — FlowCheck never sees or stores your username or password. FlowCheck only receives read-only transaction data (balances and transactions). We cannot move money or make changes to your accounts. All data is encrypted with 256-bit TLS in transit and at rest.
What banks does FlowCheck support?
FlowCheck uses Plaid, which supports over 12,000 financial institutions in the US — including Chase, Bank of America, Wells Fargo, Citi, Capital One, American Express, and virtually every credit union and regional bank. If your bank supports online login, it almost certainly works with Plaid.
How is the financial health score calculated?
The score (0–850) weighs four factors: your savings rate (how much of your income you're saving), bill payment history (overdue bills hurt the score), budget adherence (staying within your monthly budget), and emergency fund progress (how close you are to 3 months of expenses). Each factor shows you exactly what to improve.
Can I delete my data?
Yes, completely. Go to Settings → Delete Account in the app. This permanently deletes your Plaid bank connection, all transaction data, your account, and all associated data within 30 days — fully CCPA compliant. You can also email support@getflowcheck.app to request deletion.
