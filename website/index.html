Features

Everything your finances need

Six tools, one clean app. No spreadsheets, no guesswork.

🔗

Live Bank Sync

Connect checking, savings, and credit cards from 12,000+ institutions via Plaid. Balances and transactions update automatically — no manual entry.

💳

Smart Spending

Every transaction is auto-categorized. See exactly where your money goes each week, with a push alert the moment you get close to your budget limit.

📋

Bill Tracking

Add your recurring bills and FlowCheck reminds you 2 days before each one is due — via push and email — so you never pay a late fee again.

📈

Financial Health Score

A real 0–850 score built from your savings rate, bill history, budget adherence, and emergency fund. It tells you exactly what to fix — not just a number.

📊

Weekly Summaries

Every Sunday you get a clean email with your total spend, top categories, and how you're tracking vs. your goals — no login needed to read it.

🏦

Real Credit Score

Pull your actual Experian credit score inside the app. Refreshed daily, cached so it's instant. No extra apps, no "estimated" scores, no spam.

How it works

Up and running in 60 seconds

Four steps from download to your full financial picture.

1

Create your account

Sign up with your email or Apple ID. No credit card required — the core app is free to use.

2

Connect your banks

Use Plaid's secure link to connect your accounts in seconds. Your login credentials go directly to Plaid — FlowCheck never sees them.

3

See your full picture

Your balances, transactions, health score, and upcoming bills load instantly. FlowCheck keeps everything current automatically.

4

Stay on top of it

Get bill reminders, budget alerts, and payday notifications directly on your phone — so you're always in control, even when you're not in the app.

Security

Your data is safe. Full stop.

We take privacy as seriously as your bank does.

Read-only access only. FlowCheck can see your balances and transactions — nothing else. We cannot move money, make payments, or change anything in your accounts.
🔐

256-bit encryption

All data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest. Your bank credentials are handled exclusively by Plaid — we never see them.

🏦

Powered by Plaid

The same bank connection technology used by Venmo, Coinbase, and thousands of other fintech apps. Trusted by millions.

🚫

We never sell your data

Your financial data is yours. We don't sell it, share it with advertisers, or use it to train AI models — ever.

🗑️

Delete anytime

Request full deletion from Settings at any time. Every piece of your data is permanently erased within 30 days.

🔒

SSN never stored

If you check your Experian credit score, your SSN is transmitted directly to Experian over an encrypted connection and is never stored by FlowCheck.

📋

FCRA compliant

Credit score requests are consumer-initiated under FCRA § 604(a)(2) — you're pulling your own report, not being underwritten or scored for lending.
