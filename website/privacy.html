Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2025 · Effective: May 20, 2025

The short version: FlowCheck connects to your bank accounts to show you your own financial data. We use that data to run the app for you — and nothing else. We don't sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it to train AI models. You can delete your account and all your data at any time.

1. Who We Are

FlowCheck ("FlowCheck," "we," "us," or "our") is a personal finance iOS application. Our website is getflowcheck.app. You can reach us at privacy@getflowcheck.app for any privacy-related questions.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Account information: Your name, email address, and password when you register.
  • Manual entries: Bills, budgets, savings goals, and any other data you manually enter in the app.
  • Credit score identity data: If you request your Experian credit score, you will provide your name, date of birth, address, and Social Security Number. This information is transmitted to Experian and is never stored on FlowCheck's servers or in our database.

Device information

We collect your device's push notification token to send you alerts you've requested. We do not collect device identifiers for advertising purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect exclusively to:

  • Display your financial data, balances, and transaction history
  • Calculate your Financial Health Score
  • Send bill payment reminders and due-date alerts
  • Send budget overage alerts when you exceed a spending limit you've set
  • Detect direct deposits and payday arrivals and notify you
  • Send your weekly financial summary email (if enabled)
  • Display your Experian credit score (Pro plan)
  • Manage your subscription through RevenueCat
  • Authenticate you securely via Firebase
  • Respond to your support requests
We do not sell your data, share it with advertisers, use it to build advertising profiles, or use it to train machine learning models.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore, a SOC 2 Type II certified cloud database, in the United States. We use the following security practices:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3
  • All data is encrypted at rest using AES-256
  • Bank access tokens (from Plaid) are stored in a server-only Firestore collection, never in the app or client-accessible storage
  • Your Social Security Number and date of birth are never written to any database — they are used in-memory only, transmitted to Experian, and immediately discarded
  • Your credit score is cached in your Firestore document for up to 24 hours, then refreshed — only the score itself, never raw PII
  • Your RevenueCat Pro status is cached in device local storage for up to 7 days as a network-failure fallback
  • Firebase Authentication tokens use short-lived JWT tokens and require re-verification for sensitive operations

6. Email Communications

We send the following transactional emails:

  • Welcome email — sent once when you create your account
  • Bill reminders — sent 2 days before a bill you've added is due
  • Budget alerts — sent when you exceed a spending limit you've set
  • Weekly summary — sent every Sunday with your spending breakdown

Every email includes an unsubscribe link. You can also manage email preferences in Settings → Notifications, or unsubscribe from all FlowCheck emails at any time by visiting getflowcheck.app/unsubscribe.

7. Push Notifications

If you grant notification permission, FlowCheck may send push notifications for:

  • Bill due-date reminders
  • Budget overage alerts
  • Payday and direct deposit detection
  • Weekly financial summary

You can disable push notifications at any time in your iPhone Settings → Notifications → FlowCheck, or in the app under Settings → Notifications.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all of your data — including transactions, accounts, bills, goals, budgets, notifications, and credit history — is permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days. Backups may retain data for up to an additional 90 days before permanent deletion.

9. Your Rights

All users

  • Access: You can view all your data in the app at any time.
  • Deletion: Delete your account from Settings → Account → Delete Account. All data is permanently erased.
  • Correction: Update your name or email in Settings → Profile.
  • Opt-out of emails: Use the unsubscribe link in any email, or manage preferences in Settings → Notifications.

California residents (CCPA)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act, California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect and how it's used
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, email privacy@getflowcheck.app.

EEA / UK residents (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, you have rights under GDPR including access, rectification, erasure, portability, and the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority. Our lawful basis for processing your data is performance of our contract with you (providing the app service). Contact privacy@getflowcheck.app to exercise your rights.

10. Children's Privacy

FlowCheck is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at privacy@getflowcheck.app and we will delete it promptly.

11. Credit Score Data

FlowCheck allows you to manually enter a credit score you already know from your bank, credit card issuer, or credit monitoring service. FlowCheck does not retrieve credit reports or access any credit bureau data. The score you enter is stored in your account and is never shared with third parties or used for any lending, insurance, or employment purpose.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by email (if you have an account) and update the "Last updated" date above. Your continued use of FlowCheck after the effective date of any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
